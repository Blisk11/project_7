Project 7: Deploy a machine learning model online

The dataset is from this , competition

The web app is a simulation of what a customer representitive would have available when meeting with a customer

To optimize this model, please choose the following 2 parameters:

  • The average ammount of credit reimburse before default (as a percentage of the total loan)
  • The expected profit of your customer, as a percentage of their credit. (interest rate + insurance etc.)

By modifying these two parameters, we can optimise the threshold of acceptance of the model to maximise profits on the validation dataset.
